About Me

I was born in Hong Kong and came to the U.S in 2008. I went to Alaska for high school and I am now a senior at Carnegie Mellon University studying Computer Science and Economics.

During the summer of 2015, I interned at NASA Ames Research Center's Human System Interaction department as a software developer. I have also worked in the Robotics Institute as an undergraduate researcher in the Graphics Lab and volunteered at Center for Victims in Pittsburgh as a web developer. Currently, I am software developer at Bossa Nova Robotics's Data Engineering team.

I play billiards competitively and I love the sport. I won first place at University of Akron Regionals for Women's 9 Ball and second place at University of Michigan Team Championships with my CMU team in 2015. Look below for my notes on drills and practices!

In my free time, I also like cooking, playing violin, and painting with gouache. Check below for recipes and paintings that I've done!
